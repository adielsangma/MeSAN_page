About Us

The MeSAN Union is the official representative body for all students of NERIST, who are from the state of Meghalaya regardless of their tribe or religion they belong. MeSAN was founded on the year '2016' and on the 25th of February 2016, the very first Executive Body was formed. We are dedicated to providing a platform for students from MeSAN to connect with each other, and to represent their interests to the college administration and the community. Our goal is to foster a sense of community and belonging among students of MeSAN, and to provide opportunities for personal and academic growth. We offer a variety of events and activities, resources and support services, and advocacy and representation opportunities, that are specific to the students of MeSAN. Whether you're looking to get involved in campus life, find support and resources, or just have some fun, our Union is here to help. We are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment for all students, and to make sure that they have a positive and successful college experience.
